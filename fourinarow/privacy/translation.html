Translations

App Translator stores all translations you make on its servers. These translations are encrypted, and no one, not even the App Translator developpers, can read them. They can only be decrypted when you request the exact same translation again.

App Translator uses external services to process the translations you request. They may store and access your translations according to their own privacy policy.
